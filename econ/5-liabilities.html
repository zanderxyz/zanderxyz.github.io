5 Liabilities

Liabilities are the opposite side of the balance sheet from Assets. Just as are an Asset to the person receiving income from it, they are a Liability for the people paying that income.

This is a great example of how our model differs from the real world by design. Even though the customers of a company are benefiting from the arrangement through the products they buy, in our financial model we only see the downside from their perspective - the money they must pay.
