18 Low Hanging Fruit

On top of our proposed change to government budgets, if we use this model to look at other government policies there are so many that are completely counter-productive. A smart government has so many ways to improve things at little-to-no cost.

Saving

Currently policy encourages saving in pensions and ISAs using tax breaks. For each individual, saving makes sense, as it makes them richer relative to others. Considered across the whole population however, saving slows GNI growth , and in the long run makes everyone poorer.

Encouraging acts that help individuals but hurt society are exactly the opposite of what the government should be doing.
